About PAGE

We believe education is a fundamental human right and not a privilege. We believe literacy is the ability to read the world (not limited reading words). We are living in an exponential time— the socio-economic location, technological progress, climate changes, and many forces that deeply impact human wellbeing are changing at the fastest rate in the history of human civilization. These changes are affecting the marginalized people of the world, and Bangladesh is no different. PAGE works towards democratizing literacy for creating a more equitable and just Bangladesh.
